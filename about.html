Just in case you are wondering what Project 774 is, here is how it started

Project774

Inspiring Talents—Making a Difference

Emerging innovative technology defines today's era. We are living in an age when the digital and physical worlds are combining. These times require completely revolutionary approaches to education and inspiration approaches that start with catching them young. So, how do we prepare tomorrow's leaders and today's eager enthusiasts for the current "technology renaissance"? What are the best ways to prepare individuals for global challenges? How can we expand the accessibility of tech education in our country in this significant economic and social transformation period?

Project 774 aspires to inspire fresh talent in developing digital fields, as well as bring individuals together to collaborate, network, and generate long-term value throughout the 774 LGAs. Because there is so much untapped potential, Nigeria is a gold mine when it comes to the future of IT development and growth. Bridging the digital divide through the use of technology and technological infrastructure to generate a variety of job opportunities is undoubtedly the way to go in order to reverse unemployment trends. We need to rethink what skills Nigerians require in order to participate in the global digital economy.

Goals and Objectives

We are a community that shares the same goal: to assist you in developing the work and social skills that will allow you to begin and succeed in your career. When it comes to talent, Project 774 recognizes that it is equally distributed, but opportunity isn't.

Building an Inclusive Community

Fostering an environment in which participants may engage, network, and exchange different and valuable experiences.

Connecting Members with Professional Opportunities

Attract valuable professional contacts and use them as a springboard to finding a job and ascending the career ladder.

Acquiring Social and Tech Skills:

Gain a competitive advantage in the industry by developing critical and necessary work and social skills.

Professional Development Programs with Industry Partners

Get the training you need to make a difference in your community and advance in your career through programs offered in collaboration with industry leaders.

Offering Mentorship Opportunities

Encourage Technology Investment
